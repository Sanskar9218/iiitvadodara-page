    IIIT Vadodara


    FOUNDATION

    The Indian Institute of Information technology, Vadodara, Gujarat is one of the new IIITs proposed by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India under the PPP model.IIIT Vadodara got it's status of Institute of National Importance under the IIIt act 2017.It has initiated its functioning in Gandhinagar in 2013, under the mentorship of DAIICT. IIIT Vadodara has started its first batch in the academic year 2013-2014 with a strength of 36 students.At present the Institute has no mentor Institute and functions on its own.It offers courses in B.tech,M.tech and PhD.

    STUDENT LIFE

    The students have started several clubs and they are learning a lot of skills from their seniors. All the students are actively participating in the Technical and cultural activities.IIITV hosts several big events throughout the year,which mainly are:- Krieva(The cultural fest),Ventura(The sports meet),Cerebro(The technical fest) and Nabhyaan(The interhouse competition).

    NEWS & UPDATES

    Cerebro 2020

    The annual tecnical fest of IIIT Vadodara was planned between 27-29 march in the Government Engineering College(GEC)Gandhinagar.With the loved events as "The Tech Hunt","Cook a Code","Type racer",etc Cerebro 2019 was a complete success and to maintain its legacy Cerebro 2020 was awaiting,but due to the covid-19 pandemic it has been postponed until everything gets back to normal.

    Republic Day Celebration

    IIIT VADODARA celebrated Republic Day with Prof.Sarat Kumar Patra, Director, IIIT Vadodara, hoisting the National Flag at GEC Gandhinagar(The transit campus of IIITV). It was followed by various programs such as drama, singing, band performances performed by the students. Further Prof.Sarat Patra distributed prizes to the winners various sports events of Nabhyaan.

    Nabhyaan 2020

    The annual interhouse competition of IIITV was a two day event from 11-12 January 2020 hosted at SITG(The temporary hostel of IIITV).Those days were full of life and fun, all credits to the events and their organizers.Days were filled with fun competitions such as Rangoli,Box Cricket and Tug of War whereas, nights had antakshari,damsharaz,dance performances and DJ nights to entertain the students.
